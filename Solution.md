GreenPulse AI is an all-in-one carbon footprint awareness platform that automatically tracks emissions from travel, energy usage, shopping, and daily habits. It provides a personalized Carbon Score, AI-powered recommendations, daily eco-challenges, streaks, rewards, and impact visualizations. Users can scan products to view their environmental impact, compare their footprint with peers and regional averages, join community challenges, and earn points for sustainable actions. The platform converts complex sustainability data into simple, actionable insights, helping users understand, reduce, and improve their carbon footprint through measurable lifestyle changes.
